# Aevum — Staged Rollout with Measurable Exit Criteria (v3)

> The sequence is deliberately *unglamorous first*: the path to global scale
> runs through trust and inclusion infrastructure, not new currencies. Each
> stage has a **definition of done** so the staging is an accountable plan, not
> a narrative. Stages map to `App\Support\RolloutStage`.

---

## Stage 1 — Trustworthy core (the wallet people don't abandon)

**Scope:** ledger with enforced integer‑minor‑unit invariants; seven‑currency
registry (`$FOCUS` already replaced by `$MAKE`, `$PEG` split into
`$PEG`/`$PEG+`); pseudonymous Rung‑0 wallets; **social recovery**; scam/risk
scoring with sign‑time simulation; reversibility windows & escrow primitives;
offline / feature‑phone access (USSD/SMS, offline‑signed deferred settlement),
low‑literacy multi‑language voice UX.

**Exit criteria (definition of done):**

- Social recovery tested by ≥ 10,000 users across ≥ 3 continents.
- Offline transactions used in ≥ 2 pilot communities with ≥ 95% settlement
  success.
- Independent security audit of the MintEngine invariants completed and
  **published**.
- 100% of money mutations covered by total‑supply‑conservation and
  no‑negative‑balance invariant tests.

## Stage 2 — Truth and money‑in

**Scope:** Oracle domain (multi‑source, slashing, dispute games); Compliance
domain; first **licensed** fiat tunnel in one jurisdiction with real‑time
reserve attestation; `$OXY` bound to retirable registry serials; the
macro‑simulation sandbox as a **blocking** gate for any new currency or policy
change.

**Exit criteria:**

- ≥ N independent providers per high‑value feed, with slashing live.
- Carbon‑oracle dispute window ≥ the real‑world verification delay (months) with
  multi‑round escalation to human arbitration.
- One fiat tunnel licensed and attesting reserves in real time.
- No currency/policy change merges without an attached, public simulation report.

## Stage 3 — Governance and personhood

**Scope:** constitutional layer (immutable tier, timelocks, appeal paths);
anti‑plutocratic voting (personhood‑weighted for constitutional matters,
conviction/quadratic for parameters, delegation for the apathetic);
user‑sovereign ethical filter with contestable labels; Rung‑1→Rung‑3 identity
ladder with pluralistic providers; `$UNA` Rung‑1 probationary dividend pool —
partial coverage, honestly labelled.

**Exit criteria:**

- ConstitutionalParameter changes provably require high quorum + timelock.
- ≥ 2 independent personhood providers integrated, each with published
  false‑accept/false‑reject profiles and a slashing bond.
- Personhood denials produce explanations and an appeal with a bound turnaround.
- Rung‑1 issuance demonstrably capped at the constitutional fraction.

## Stage 4 — Scale and intelligence

**Scope:** L2 topology (app‑specific rollups, DA layer, sponsored/abstracted
fees); cross‑chain bridge mesh with light‑client verification and the ethical
filter applied at ingress; the AI **proposer** (Tier‑1 advisory only) with its
full public audit trail; tunnels expanded jurisdiction by jurisdiction.

**Exit criteria:**

- Sustained throughput target met on L2 with periodic settlement proofs.
- Every Tier‑1 proposal records its input vector, model hash, and rationale,
  publicly queryable.
- Bridge ingress runs every asset through the ethical filter before minting.

---

At every stage the **Trade‑off Register** and the **maturity labels** are
updated, and every automatic action and governance decision leaves an immutable,
queryable record.
