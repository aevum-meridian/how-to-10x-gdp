**7.4 Update Integrity.** Distributions that include or replace the auto-update mechanism must enforce dual-signature verification and transparency-log inclusion proofs as required by Protocol #9. Distributions that disable update integrity verification are not licensed under this License.

**7.5 Coordinated Disclosure.** Distributors who discover security vulnerabilities in Covered Files agree to act in accordance with the coordinated-disclosure policy referenced in Protocol #10, including a maximum 90-day embargo and good-faith engagement with the upstream maintainers.
